Greeting Cards 2.0

– The Future of Social Expressions –

In an information-rich world, the scarce resource is attention.

– Herbert Simon, 1969 –

Team papercherry is developing the greeting card of the future. We're working with HP to envision and prototype how people will acknowledge each other on special occasions in an increasingly digital world.

Design

During the summer semester, we will be developing a prototype based off of the insights we gained in the spring semester's research phase. We interviewed participants, conducted contextual inquiries, and visited card stores for guerilla research.

Process

The project started off with a kick-off meeting in January. We worked with HP and discussed the various needs and goals of the projects.

Team

papercherry is the combination of 5 MHCI students at Carnegie Mellon University. They are sponsored by HP.
